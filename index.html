Welcome to our school

At Damtops International Schools, we are proud of our Christian foundation, which shapes everything we do. Our commitment to Christ-centered education ensures that our students not only excel academically but also grow in their faith and moral integrity. We believe that each child is a unique creation of God, endowed with talents and abilities that we are dedicated to helping them discover and develop.

Mission Statement

  • To inculcate in the scholars the pursuit of Godliness, integrity and excellence.
  • To lay a foundation for the scholars to develop the qualities of hard work, initiatives and friendship.
  • To nurture scholars to develop leadership qualities and contribute positively to the society.
  • To offer qualitative and functional primary and secondary education.

Vision Statement

To provide a first class learning environment
Where students are groomed in the fear of God
As well as nurtured mentally, physically and psychologically
To function effectively at the topmost height in any society.
