REHOBOTH INTERNATIONAL SCHOOLS

Rehoboth Int’l Secondary School, Nodu-Okpuno, also known as REHOBOTH SOLOMON’S PORCH, is more than a school. It is God’s own vision founded on the solid rock of Job 32:8. This vision was born out of a passionate heart for God’s guidance and leading in showing us the best way to affect this generation for good. An encounter from two powerful books, Wisdom from Above from Bishop David Abioye and 30 Minutes Motivation for Teachers from an unknown Author granted me access into the mind of God in proffering a solution to the decaying standard and outputs of our educational system. From the books it was crystal clear that the goal of education is understanding, for an applied knowledge in making the world a better place. But Job 32:8 has it that God is the source of the supernatural intelligence (understanding) which the world seeks after.

Having found this linking truth, I enquired of God of how to make it known to the world as we have solely relied on our expertise and he said through a school system. Burning with the zeal to demonstrate the realities of this insight from God’s word. I ventured into deep research and craving for empowerment to make this a reality. As far back as August 2011, REHOBOTH LESSON CENTER for JAMB, SSCE, JSSCE, BASIC 6 was launched with about 30 candidates with a mandate of making the best out of these candidates. Our services also extended into career talks, consultation, Science Exhibitions etc. This Lesson Centre grew tremendously and it ran from 2011 inception year bearing Rehoboth Solomon’s Porch Educational Centre. We recorded huge successes from our candidates and there was urgent demand for a permanent school. This birthed the primary and Nursery section which got her approval in 2020. In 2013 the proposed school was registered with the CAC as a full pledge educational enterprise while still running as a Nursery and Primary School.

Arguably, the Lesson Centre started in a rented three bungalows located in the defunct Dike Nworah’s Estate at Nodu- Okpuno as the population grew with the speed of light. Our educational vision also gave birth to REHOBOTH REACH-OUT EDUCATIONAL FOUNDATION with the sole mandate to give educational value – chain services in scholarships, career talks, etc with the collective efforts from well meaning Nigerians within and those in the Diaspora.

In 2014, we launched out with a Day – care service at the popular Kwata function with the name REHOBOTH HOME OF CARE which later collapse into forming the main school in our parents’ location at Nodu-Okpuno in September 2015 with creche’ and Nursery (1)oneclass coupled with Basic one having secured some facilities by the help of God and having those things which was not there from the beginning.

As God had said years ago that He will make us a household name. In the 2021/2022 session, precisely September 2021 the secondary arm sprang up with just 4 students two of which were on scholarship. At the end of the session, we could boast of 9 students. The journey into the second session came with a lot of uncertainty considering the disappointing number in population.

The school is under the management of Pastor Eze Philip Onyenweuwa who doubles as the Principal also. It is driven by a formidable team made up of the PTA, the Teachers and God. To God alone be all the glory.

Thank you for visiting and may God bless you.
