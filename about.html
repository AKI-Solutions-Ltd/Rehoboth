DAMTOPS INTERNATIONAL SCHOOLS

Welcome to Damtops International Schools, home of Damtops International Secondary School and Damtops Nursery and Primary School. As the Principal of Damtops International Secondary School (D.I.S.S), it is my great pleasure to extend a warm and heartfelt welcome to you. Our school is a place where faith, learning and character come together to create a nurturing and inspiring environment for our students.

At Damtops International Schools, we are proud of our Christian foundation, which shapes everything we do. Our commitment to Christ-centered education ensures that our students not only excel academically but also grow in their faith and moral integrity. We believe that each child is a unique creation of God, endowed with talents and abilities that we are dedicated to helping them discover and develop.

Our excellent teaching process is at the heart of our mission. Our dedicated and highly qualified faculty members are passionate about education and are committed to providing the highest standard of teaching. They employ innovative methods and personalized approaches to ensure that each student reaches their full potential. Our academic programs are rigorous and designed to challenge an inspire students to think critically, solve problems and engage with the world around them.

Integrity and moral excellence are cornerstones of our community. At Damtops International Schools, we uphold the highest standards of honesty and ethical behavior. We do not tolerate malpractice in any form and are committed to fostering an environment where students learn the importance of integrity, responsibility and respect. Our goal is to equip our students with the moral compass they need to navigate the complexities of life with confidence and grace.

We invite you to explore our website to learn more about the vibrant and dynamic community at Damtops International Schools. Whether you are a prospective student, a parent, or a member of the wider community, we hope you will find the information you need and be inspired by what we offer. We look forward to partnering with you in the educational journey of our students, as we strive to honor God in all that we do.

Thank you for visiting and may God bless you.

Sincerely,

Oduntan Samuel Oluwole
Principal, D.I.S
